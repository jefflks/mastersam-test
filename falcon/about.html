ABOUT US

Who is Silverlake?

Silverlake began as a specialist in the replacement of legacy to large-scale core banking systems and is regarded today as one of the most significant Banking and Financial solutions and services provider in the Asia Pacific region. Silverlake has over 100 global and local clients that include 40% of the top 20 largest banks in Southeast Asia.

With new economic spaces being formed in the Digital Economy, Silverlake’s clients’ demands are also changing. In order to add value to the business relationships, Silverlake has further amped up its forward-looking solutions to ensure that clients remain competitive. This is the single most important reason why Silverlake remains a highly relevant, integral partner in the fast-changing technological realm.

Silverlake aims to be the dominant system integrator of core banking systems solutions, and a leading innovator in software engineering and business solutions for the Digital Economy. The company leverages on this clearly defined strategy to generate value for clients and stakeholders.

This commitment is derived through the “Symmetry and Group Theory” subscribed by the company and practiced by employees in all activities, be it systems design, development, implementation, integration, consultancy or support services.

Going forward in the Digital Economy, Silverlake is poised to grow with its clients vis-à-vis complete, innovative IT solutions. As one of the largest and most successful IT solutions provider in Asia Pacific, Silverlake is recognised for its significant contributions to IT developments, culminating in many prestigious international awards.

Currently, Silverlake’s strong growth path has seen the company employ more than 1,200 people in Silverlake and related entities located in Asia Pacific, Middle East, Europe and USA.

Why Choose Us?

Silverlake Group is the largest IT solutions provider in Asia Pacific, especially for many leading financial institutions across the region. More importantly, Silverlake’s innovative IT solutions are complemented by some of the most thorough customer service programs in the industry.
