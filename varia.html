    VariA Multi-factor Authentication
    Service Platform(MFASP)


    VariA Multi-factor Authentication Service Platform(MFASP) is a solution that covers multitude of security features. The most cost-effective solution, supporting various authentication methods such as Push, TOTP and QR code and the apps are secured with password, PIN and fingerprint

    A multi-factor authentication solution that acts as a formidable line of defence against unauthorized system access across an enterprise




    Why VariA – Multi-Factor Authentication Service Platform?

    Passwords accounted for 81% of data breaches in the past few years. Over the years, passwords and password encryption methods have become more complex, but so have the dexterity of hackers. Passwords can be obtained illegally through phishing, brute forcing or legally by being shared by users for convenience. This way, organisations are largely clueless when a password was compromised and access is granted to all who enter it. In today’s thread landscape, relying on just a username and password is therefore no longer sufficient or advisable.

    CAPABILITIES

    “MFASP OFFERS ONE DEPLOYMENT, MULTIPLE CHANNELS”

    MOBILE APPS AUTHENTICATION FACTOR

    Mobile apps equipped as the most cost-eﬀective solution, supporting various authentication methods such as Push, TOTP and QR code and the apps are secured with password, PIN and ﬁngerprint

    ACCESS CONTROL

    Single point of control to disable a user’s access instead of having to disable access in all applications

    FUTURE-PROOF

    Any new MFA mechanisms (e.g. Facial recognition as MFA) or technology (more secure cryptography algorithms) need only be upgraded in MFASP rather than in all applications

    ANYTIME AND ANYWHERE

    Based on latest TOTP mechanism that is trusted and used by major companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Amazon, LastPass, etc. MFASP does not require any mobile or network connectivity between device and server, so it can be used even travelling or when mobile connectivity is limited

    APPLICATION SUPPORT

    Supports both web-based and client-based applications. Simple integration using RESTful APIs
