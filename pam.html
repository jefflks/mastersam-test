    Privilege Access Management (PAM)


    Organizations seeking to mitigate the risk of data breaches and insider threats, which are often associated with stolen, compromised or misused privileged credentials, resort to Privileged Access Management (PAM) tools. Increasingly, regulatory and compliance standards require, explicitly or implicitly, the implementation of PAM controls over privileged users within an IT enterprise. The absence or inadequacy of such controls often result in failed audits and eventually culpable in business disruptions following an unnoticed cyber attack.

    MasterSAM has been in PAM domain since 2004 and is being commercial deployed to customers across the ASEAN geography.

    Capabilities

    ACCOUNT DISCOVERY & SESSION MANAGEMENT
    • To effectively secure your digital assets, it is important to be able to identify where your privileged accounts are before they can be managed. To put it simply, you can't secure what you can't see
    • MasterSAM's discovery and onboarding features allows your organization to increase productivity and visibility by detecting digital assets from a central console. Detected digital assets can then be onboarded and policies applied to provide seamless management of new assets.
    PRIVILEGED ELEVATION & DELEGATION MANAGEMENT
    • MasterSAM PAM allows for privilege escalation by providing automated privileged rights assignment and demotion to user own ID , without involving passwords.
    • Adhering to Just-in-Time PAM methodology, the solution eradicates standing privileges and granting privileges only on-demand and ensuring that persons have the privilege only for a specific purpose for the duration they actually require it.
    SECRETS MANAGEMENT
    • Credentials (such as passwords, OAuth tokens and SSH keys), and secrets for software and machines are programmatically managed, stored, and retrieved through APIs and SDKs.
    • MasterSAM supports API and SDK for applications to call for passwords instead of relying on embedded or hard coded credentials with minimal code change. Functions specific to secrets management also support dynamic environments and provide support for RPA platforms.
    MASTERSAM ROBOTIC PROCESSING AUTOMATION
    • MasterSAM ZIETRA is launched to introduce RPA as an enterprise-wide practice to improve business process automation in privileged access management.
    • It offers AI-assisted behavioral detection, prevention and reviews to address fraud detection and prevent unauthorised intervention in the systems.
    MASTERSAM ON CLOUD
    • MasterSAM enables organizations to protect cloud resources by providing comprehensive Privileged Access Management solutions for securing privileged accounts and credentials at every stage of the cloud journey.
    • Regardless of whether an organization has fully embraced the cloud or is just starting the journey, it is essential to implement robust privilege management policies to protect cloud assets. MasterSAM provides solutions for Azure, AWS, Google and other cloud providers.
