Job Openings

The Senior Software Developer will develop, enhance, and maintain our suite of products.

You will frequently participate in brainstorming sessions to tackle complicated technical challenges faced by the customers, and also to design the best features to assist customers to fulfil their operational, audit, and compliance needs.

Essential:
  • Minimum degree in any relevant IT field
  • 6+ years of working experience in a related field
  • Able to communicate proficiently in English in both oral and written skills
  • Experience in Java programming language, libraries, and tooling, e.g. Hibernate, Tomcat, Spring, Eclipse etc
  • Self-motivated, able to multi-task and work with minimum supervision
  • Personable, helpful and professional attitude
Desirable
  • Experience in .Net Programming Language
  • Knowledge of Security Concepts e.g. PKI, Hashing, Symmetric and Asymmetric Encryption etc
  • Experience in PAM or Security Related environments
  • Experience in Financial Institutions related environments
  • Comfortable with multi-disciplinary work, e.g. architecture, pre-sales, hands-on coding
  • Adaptive and dynamic, able to take up challenges to perform R&D in non-familiar fields of business
  • Experience with cloud technologies
Job Type: Permanent

Office Location: Phileo Damansara, Petaling Jaya & Megan Avenue II, KL

Working hours: 9 AM – 5.30 PM (Mon – Fri) (based on the project, at times one may be required to work beyond these hours)

Dress code: Mon – Thur: Business Formals, Friday: Business casuals (if not visiting customer location)

Travel: Yes (within Malaysia at the customer location and overseas across SEA region for short durations – on need basis)
